Ingredients:
- porcini mushrooms; roasted squash; onions, carrots, celery; beans; aubergine; mushrooms; creme fraiche; vegetarian cheese; spinach; lasagna sheets; cheddar; cherry tomatoes; herbs; red wine

Preparation:
1. Roast squash. 2. Cook vegetables with wine and beans. 3. Prepare ragù with mushrooms and creme fraiche. 4. Layer pasta, ragù, spinach, white sauce. 5. Top with cheese and tomatoes. 6. Bake ~45 min and rest 30 min.
