Ingredients:
- minced meat; onions; chilli garlic sauce; tomato puree; lasagna sheets; cheddar & mozzarella; butter, flour, milk; oregano; chilli flakes; mushrooms optional

Preparation:
1. Cook meat with onions and tomato sauce. 2. Prepare white sauce. 3. Layer pasta, meat sauce, white sauce, cheese. 4. Repeat. 5. Bake until top is browned.
