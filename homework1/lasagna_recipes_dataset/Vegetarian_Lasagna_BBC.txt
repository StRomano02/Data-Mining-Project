Ingredients:
- lasagna sheets; peppers; aubergines; onions; garlic; canned tomatoes; tomato purée; white wine; basil; butter; flour; milk; mozzarella

Preparation:
1. Sauté vegetables. 2. Make tomato sauce. 3. Prepare white sauce. 4. Layer pasta, sauce, vegetables, white sauce. 5. Top with mozzarella. 6. Bake until golden.
