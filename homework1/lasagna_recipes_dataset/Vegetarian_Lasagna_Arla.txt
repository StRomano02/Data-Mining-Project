Ingredients:
- red pepper, courgette, onion, shallots, garlic, chilli; tinned tomatoes; stock cube; spinach; basil; lasagna sheets; butter; flour; milk; nutmeg; salt & pepper

Preparation:
1. Cook vegetables. 2. Add tomatoes, stock, herbs. 3. Prepare white sauce. 4. Layer pasta, veggie mixture, white sauce, spinach. 5. Repeat and bake until set.
