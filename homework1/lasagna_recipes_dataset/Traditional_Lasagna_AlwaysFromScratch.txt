Ingredients:
- lasagna sheets; ground beef; pork; onions; garlic; tomato sauce; tomato paste; herbs; ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan; eggs; salt & pepper

Preparation:
1. Brown meat with onions and garlic. 2. Add tomato sauce and paste with herbs. 3. Mix ricotta with eggs and Parmesan. 4. Layer pasta, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, mozzarella. 5. Repeat layers. 6. Bake until golden and bubbling.
