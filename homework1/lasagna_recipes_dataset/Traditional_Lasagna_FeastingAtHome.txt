Ingredients:
- lasagna noodles; ground beef; Italian sausage; onions; garlic; tomato paste; canned tomatoes; herbs; ricotta; Parmesan; mozzarella; eggs; salt & pepper

Preparation:
1. Brown meat with onions and garlic. 2. Add tomato paste and tomatoes with herbs. 3. Mix ricotta with eggs and Parmesan. 4. Layer pasta, sauce, ricotta mixture, mozzarella. 5. Repeat layers and bake until bubbly.
