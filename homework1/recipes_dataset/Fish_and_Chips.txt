Ingredients:
- thick white fish fillets (cod, haddock, pollock)
- flour
- cornstarch
- baking powder
- cold dark beer
- cold sparkling water
- salt and pepper
- potatoes
- vegetable oil for frying

Preparation:
1. Prepare batter with flour, cornstarch, baking powder, salt, pepper, beer, sparkling water; chill 30-60 mins.
2. Cut potatoes, rinse, parboil 3-4 mins, drain, chill.
3. Heat oil 350°F, fry chips 2 mins, drain.
4. Pat fish dry, coat with flour, dip in batter.
5. Fry fish 6-8 mins until golden, drain.
6. Increase oil to 400°F, fry chips again until golden. Serve with tartar sauce.
