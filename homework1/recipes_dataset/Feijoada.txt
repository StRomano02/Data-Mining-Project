Ingredients:
- dry black beans (soaked overnight)
- ham hocks
- diced ham
- bacon
- onion and green onions
- garlic
- bay leaves
- ground coriander
- salt and pepper
- chopped cilantro and parsley

Preparation:
1. Saut√© onions, green onions, garlic until soft.
2. Add drained beans, cover with water, simmer 2 hrs until beans tender.
3. Simmer ham hocks separately, slice meat, add to beans.
4. Bake diced ham, bacon, remaining onion until crispy, add to beans.
5. Add bay leaves, coriander, salt, pepper; simmer 30 mins.
6. Stir in cilantro and parsley, serve hot with rice and orange slices.
