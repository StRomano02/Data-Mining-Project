Ingredients:
- olive oil
- chicken pieces
- pork ribs (or rabbit)
- garlic
- tomatoes
- flat green beans
- lima beans
- sweet paprika
- saffron threads
- short-grain rice
- salt
- water

Preparation:
1. Heat oil, brown chicken and pork.
2. Add green beans, saut√©, then garlic and lima beans.
3. Add paprika, tomato, saffron.
4. Pour water, simmer 15-20 mins.
5. Add rice, spread evenly, cook without stirring.
6. Raise heat briefly for socarrat, let rest, serve.
