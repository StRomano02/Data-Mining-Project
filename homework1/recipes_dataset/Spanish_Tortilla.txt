Ingredients:
- potatoes
- olive oil
- salt and pepper
- sweet paprika (optional)
- onion
- eggs
- parsley or chives (optional)

Preparation:
1. Heat olive oil, sauté sliced potatoes with salt and paprika.
2. Add onion, cook until golden.
3. Drain excess oil, cool slightly.
4. Beat eggs, fold in potatoes and onions.
5. Heat skillet, pour mixture, cook edges set.
6. Bake at 350°F 5 mins until puffed and golden. Cut and serve.
