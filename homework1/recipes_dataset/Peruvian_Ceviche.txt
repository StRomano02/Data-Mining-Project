Ingredients:
- white fish fillets
- shrimp
- fresh lime juice
- sliced red onion
- minced garlic
- minced chili pepper
- chopped cilantro
- salt and pepper
- boiled potatoes, lettuce leaves (for serving)

Preparation:
1. Boil potatoes, cool, set aside for garnish.
2. Soak red onion in warm water briefly, drain.
3. Blend lime juice with cilantro, celery, cumin (optional).
4. Stir in garlic, chili, season with salt and pepper.
5. Combine fish and shrimp in marinade, sit 1 hr until opaque.
6. Stir in drained onion, serve on lettuce with boiled potatoes.
