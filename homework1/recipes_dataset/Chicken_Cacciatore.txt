Ingredients:
- chicken pieces (thighs or mixed)
- olive oil
- onion
- celery
- bell pepper
- garlic
- mushrooms
- dried oregano
- thyme
- parsley
- red pepper flakes
- crushed tomatoes
- red wine
- salt and pepper

Preparation:
1. Season and brown chicken in olive oil, then remove.
2. Saut√© onion, celery, bell pepper, mushrooms, and garlic until tender.
3. Add wine, cook down slightly, then pour in crushed tomatoes.
4. Return chicken, cover, and simmer 30 minutes until cooked and sauce thickened.
5. Adjust seasoning, garnish with parsley, and serve.
