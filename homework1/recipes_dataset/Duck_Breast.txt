Ingredients:
- 2 duck breasts (magret)
- shallot
- red wine
- duck or veal demi-glace
- salt
- pepper

Preparation:
1. Score skin, season with salt and pepper.
2. Cook skin-side down in cold skillet ~8 mins, flip, sear briefly, finish to medium-rare.
3. Remove duck, keep warm.
4. Saut√© shallot in pan fat, add wine, reduce by half, add demi-glace, reduce to sauce.
5. Slice duck, serve with sauce.
