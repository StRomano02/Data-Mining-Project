Ingredients:
- lasagna noodles
- olive oil
- ground beef
- Italian sausage
- canned tomato sauce
- sliced mushrooms
- garlic salt
- dried oregano, thyme, and basil
- salt and black pepper
- ricotta cheese
- eggs
- grated Parmesan
- shredded mozzarella

Preparation:
1. Brown the beef and sausage in olive oil, stir in tomato sauce, mushrooms, and seasonings, and simmer.
2. Boil noodles until al dente and drain.
3. Mix ricotta with eggs and Parmesan.
4. Layer meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella, repeat layers finishing with mozzarella.
5. Bake at 350Â°F for about 90 minutes. Let rest 10-15 minutes before serving.
