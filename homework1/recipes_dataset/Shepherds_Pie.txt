Ingredients:
- potatoes
- butter
- onion
- mixed vegetables
- ground meat (lamb or beef)
- beef broth
- Worcestershire sauce
- salt and pepper

Preparation:
1. Boil potatoes, mash with butter, salt, pepper.
2. Sauté onion and carrots in butter.
3. Add meat, cook until browned, drain fat.
4. Stir in Worcestershire and broth, simmer until slightly reduced.
5. Mix in vegetables, spread in baking dish, top with mashed potatoes.
6. Bake 400°F 30 mins until top browned, edges bubbly.
