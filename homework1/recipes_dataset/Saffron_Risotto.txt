Ingredients:
- chicken stock (kept warm)
- olive oil
- onion
- butter
- Arborio rice
- saffron threads
- dry white wine
- grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
- salt and pepper
- (optional parsley and extra butter)

Preparation:
1. Heat oil and butter, saut√© finely chopped onion until softened.
2. Add rice and toast briefly.
3. Steep saffron in warm stock or wine and add to rice.
4. Pour in white wine and stir until absorbed.
5. Gradually add warm stock, stirring constantly, until rice is creamy and al dente (~18-20 mins).
6. Remove from heat, stir in Parmesan, optional butter and parsley, season, and serve immediately.
