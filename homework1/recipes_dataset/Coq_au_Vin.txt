Ingredients:
- olive oil
- diced pancetta or bacon
- chicken pieces
- onions
- garlic
- cognac
- red wine
- chicken broth
- tomato paste
- balsamic vinegar
- sugar
- fresh thyme
- bay leaf
- carrots
- cremini mushrooms
- butter
- flour

Preparation:
1. Cook pancetta until crisp, remove.
2. Brown chicken, remove.
3. Sauté onions and garlic.
4. Deglaze with cognac, add wine, broth, tomato paste, vinegar, sugar, thyme, bay, simmer 15 mins.
5. Return chicken and carrots, simmer 30 mins.
6. Sauté mushrooms, mix butter and flour, stir into sauce.
7. Return chicken, mushrooms, pancetta to warm, serve.
