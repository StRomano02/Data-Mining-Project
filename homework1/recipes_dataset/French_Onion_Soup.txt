Ingredients:
- yellow or red onions
- butter and olive oil
- sugar
- beef stock
- dry vermouth or white wine
- bay leaves
- fresh thyme
- salt and pepper
- French bread slices
- grated Gruyère cheese

Preparation:
1. Thinly slice onions.
2. Sauté onions in butter and oil until very soft, add sugar and salt, cook ~30-40 mins until deep brown.
3. Add garlic, cook 1 min.
4. Deglaze with wine or vermouth, scrape browned bits.
5. Add stock, bay leaves, thyme; simmer 30 mins, season.
6. Toast bread, top with Gruyère, broil until bubbly.
7. Serve soup in bowls with cheese-topped bread.
